## Accessing Your Account

To log in:

1. Go to `https://dashboard.mwp-coub.com`
2. Click **Log In**
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click **Sign In**

If you signed up via Google or another provider, use the appropriate login option.

## Lost your password?

Click **Forgot password?** to reset it via email.
