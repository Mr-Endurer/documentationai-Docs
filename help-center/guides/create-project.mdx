## Start a New Project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Visit `https://dashboard.mwp-coub.com` and sign in with your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Project'">
    On the dashboard, click the **New Project** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Fill in project name and optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Create Project** to finish and view your project space.
  </Step>
</Steps>
