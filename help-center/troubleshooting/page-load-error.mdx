---
title: Page Won't Load
description: Troubleshoot mwP Сoub dashboard load issues.
---

## Issue

- Dashboard stuck loading
- Blank or error message displayed

## Possible Causes

- Browser cache issues
- Internet connection
- Ad-blocker interference

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reload and Clear Cache">
    ````
    Hard refresh (`Ctrl+F5` or `Cmd+Shift+R`).
    Clear browser cache and cookies for mwp-coub.com.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Ad-blockers">
    Disable extensions that may block dashboard scripts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try Another Browser">
    Open mwP Сoub in Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.
  </Step>
</Steps>
